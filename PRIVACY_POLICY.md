# Privacy Policy for Lokalog

Effective date: 2026-06-05

This Privacy Policy explains how Lokalog collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

Lokalog may process the following data on your device:

- Precise location data (foreground and background) to detect when you are near saved job locations and to log work visits.
- Saved location records you enter (for example, customer/location name and address details).
- Visit log entries generated by app behavior (for example, timestamp, location coordinates, confirmation/auto-log status, and confidence values).
- Optional device/app usage diagnostics when you explicitly enable related debug tools and grant Usage Access permission on Android.

## 2. How We Use Information

Lokalog uses data to:

- Match your current position to saved locations.
- Detect dwell time at a location and create job logs.
- Show reminders/notifications related to logging events.
- Let you review, delete, and share log entries.
- Provide optional debug and battery/usage diagnostics in the app.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- Data is stored locally on your device using app-local storage.
- Lokalog does not require an account and does not upload your core logs to a backend server by default.
- You can delete entries from within the app. Some system-level backups (if enabled on your device) may retain older snapshots outside the app's direct control.

## 4. Permissions

Lokalog may request these Android permissions:

- Location permissions (coarse/fine/background) for geofencing and visit logging.
- Notification permission to deliver log reminders.
- Usage Access permission only for optional debug battery/usage diagnostics.

You can manage or revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

## 5. Sharing

When you use the Share feature, Lokalog sends selected log text to Android's system share sheet so you can choose where to send it. Lokalog does not control how third-party apps handle shared content.

## 6. Third Parties

Lokalog does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell personal data.

The app may rely on platform services (Android/Google Play Services) for location/geofencing behavior. Those platform providers process data under their own privacy terms.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Lokalog is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data stored by the app. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 9. Your Choices

You can:

- Disable tracking in the app.
- Revoke permissions in system settings.
- Delete saved locations and logs from within the app.
- Uninstall the app to remove local app data (subject to any device backup behavior).

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.

## 11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

- Email: support@lokalog.app
